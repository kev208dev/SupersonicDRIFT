    About

    NEON APEX is a lightweight browser racing game with drift handling, lap timing, unlockable cars, and ranking hooks.

    How to Play

    On mobile, acceleration is automatic. Use the joystick to steer, drift through corners, chain a double drift, and boost on straight roads.

    • Mobile: Joystick steer, Drift slide, Double Drift chained turn, Boost burst.
    • PC: W/Arrow Up accelerate, A/D steer, S brake, Shift drift, Ctrl/Alt/Space boost or item, Z/X swap item, R respawn, / rear view, Tab records, Enter chat.

    Privacy Policy

    NEON APEX may store basic gameplay settings, nickname, score records, and leaderboard data to provide core game features. Analytics and advertising tools may be added in the future to improve the service and support development. No sensitive personal information is required to play the game.

    How to Play

    Learn the core racing controls.

    Mobile Controls

    Joystick steers the car. Acceleration is automatic on mobile.

    Use Drift, Double Drift, and Boost buttons to master corners and straights.

    PC Controls

    W / Arrow Up accelerate. A/D or arrows steer. S / Arrow Down brakes.

    Shift drifts (hold through corners). Ctrl, Alt, or Space fires boost or item. Z/X cycles items. R respawns at the centerline. / shows rear view. Tab shows records. Enter opens chat.

    Race Tips

    Drift before tight corners, double drift for sharp turns, and boost after exits.
